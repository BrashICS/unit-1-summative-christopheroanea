Who is Dennis Ritchie?

Dennis Ritchie is an American Computer who created the C and B programming language with the help of Ken Thompson. When Ritchie was born in Bronxville, New York, his dad was already working for Bell labs soon to which Ritchie would join him. Bell Labs is where Ritchie met Ken Thompson and ended up creating one of the most important programming languages.
